About the Trainer

Built on
Discipline.
Driven by Results.

I didn't build this brand on hype. I built it on showing up — every single day — when it's hard, when it's early, when nobody's watching. That's what Just Right Coaching & Training is about.

As a NASM Certified Personal Trainer based in Dedham, MA, I specialize in cutting through the noise and getting you real results. My approach is simple: move with purpose, train with intensity, and commit to the process. Whether you're training in-person at F45 Dedham or working with me remotely, you'll get programming that challenges you and a coach who holds you accountable.

This isn't a quick fix. This is a lifestyle built one rep, one session, one day at a time.
